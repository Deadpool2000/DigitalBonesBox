Fibula - Detailed Information

What is the Fibula?

The fibula is a long, thin bone located on the lateral side of the tibia in the lower leg. It runs parallel to the tibia and helps to stabilize the ankle and support the muscles of the lower leg.

Function

The primary function of the fibula is to provide support and stability to the ankle and lower leg. It also serves as an attachment point for muscles and ligaments.
