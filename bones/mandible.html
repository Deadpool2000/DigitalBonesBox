Mandible - Detailed Information

What is the Mandible?

The mandible, or lower jawbone, is the largest and strongest bone of the face. It forms the lower jaw and holds the lower teeth in place.

Function

The mandible supports the lower teeth and is involved in the movement of the jaw for chewing and speaking.

Key Features

  • Body
  • Ramus
  • Angle
  • Mandibular Notch
